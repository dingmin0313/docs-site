# CA Brightside Community Edition : Additional Resources

CA Brightside provides you with the following resources to help you use the product.

Resource Description

CA Brightside Slack workspace

Communicate with your peers and CA Technologies subject matter experts. You must create an account to join, view, and participate in the CA Brightside Slack workspace.

Important! CA Technologies does not officially support Slack workspaces. They are managed completely by the members of the Slack workspace community.

CA Brightside subreddit

Communicate with your peers and CA Technologies subject matter experts. You must create an account to participate in the CA Brightside subreddit.

Important! CA Technologies does not officially support subreddit forums. They are managed completely by the members of the subreddit community.

CA Brightside Compatibility Matrix View information about CA Brightside 1.0 and CA Brightside Community Edition compatibilities.

Connect with CA Brightside Information Engineers

When you have a question or want to report an issue that relates to our CA Brightside documentation, add your comments to the bottom of any article in this DocOps Platform and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

CA Technologies YouTube channel

Watch videos about CA Brightside, other CA Technologies products, and more!

Mainframe Product Portfolio Read the latest information about other CA Technologies mainframe products.
